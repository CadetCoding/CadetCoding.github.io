THE BACKUP: if / else

What if your "if" statement is False? We use else to tell the computer what to do instead. It's like a Plan B!

💡 PLAN B RULE:
The else: does NOT get a math check. It just catches everything else.
Make sure it is lined up perfectly under the if!
